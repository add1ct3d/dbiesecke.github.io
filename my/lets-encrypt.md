## lego
### Argument example
 * **Command with arguments**: `~/lego --email="username@gmail.com" --domains="yourdomain.de" run`
 * **Description**: Create SSL certs for following Domain
 * **Example CRON**: `~/lego --email="username@gmail.com" --domains="yourdomain.de" run`
